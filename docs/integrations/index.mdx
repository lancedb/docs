LanceDB plugs into the rest of your AI stack. Use the sections below to jump straight into the guides that matter for your workflow.

### Embeddings
Work with providers such as OpenAI, Cohere, Hugging Face, Gemini, VoyageAI, AWS Bedrock, and more to generate vectors that drop directly into LanceDB.

### Rerankers
Tune search quality with Cohere, ColBERT, VoyageAI, Answer.AI, and other rerankers for both semantic and hybrid retrieval.

### Frameworks
Wire up your application stack with integrations for LangChain, LlamaIndex, Genkit, Kiln, Meta's Synthetic Data Kit, and other popular orchestration frameworks.

### Data platforms
Move data in and out of LanceDB using DuckDB, pandas, Polars, DLT, PhiData, and additional data engineering tools.
